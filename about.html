Cracticusly

CMY

Cracticusly, also known as Moony, is a Southeast Asian Artist.

They predominantly create fanwork under the name "Cracticusly" on Tumblr and youtube.

With a background in animation and game design, Moony likes making animatics as well as rough concept sketches. However, they do also create physical merchandise like keychains and stickers! Moony is often found lurking in the Splatoon and indie-game fandoms like Transistor, Hollow Knight, Rain World.

They are currently most obsessed with Hermitcraft (minecraft youtube)! :3c
