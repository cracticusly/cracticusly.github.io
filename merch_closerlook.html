Introduction

Moony adheres to the fan-merch policies of the fandoms they produce physical goods of, including limiting the number of products to small local distribution, as well as still respecting the copyright of both the original work and fan-made content.

Moony will never use the unethical AI image generators to create fan-art. They will also not allow any purchases of their artwork to be included into learning data-set for training of AI.

Sleepy knight keychain

This 3x3.5cm acrylic keychain is based on the "little ghost" player characetr from Hollow Knight, a game developed by Team Cherry. The keychain shows the character resting on a bench, about to fall asleep.

This keychain is double-sided with unique designs on each side. The background is partially transparent, so you can pretend the little ghost is resting in any location you bring it to.

Iterator shaker

These 4x4cm acrylic shaker keychains are based on NPC characters, the "Iterators" from Rain World. These shakers contain various trinkets that moves when shaken.

The shakers are double-sided but the back of the shaker is just a flat image to show the back of the iterators' rooms.

Looks to the Moon's shaker contains the rivulet slugcat and 4 neutrons.

5 Pebble's shaker contains the artificer slugcat and 5 pearls.

Desert duo keychain

This 4x4.5cm acrylic keychain is based on the Roleplay characters of Grain and GoodTimesWithScar during their participation in 3rd Life SMP-- a short competitive, gimmick-based minecraft series.

This keychain is double-sided with the same deisgn on both sides.

Fe3h stickers

This stickers are based on Fire Emblem: 3 Houses. They are mostly characters from the Golden Deer house.

Assorted mini stickers

These are some assorted transparent stickers based on various minecraft youtube/twitch series, including QSMP,Hermitcraft and the life-SMP series. There are also some Sky:COTL and emoji stickers in the pile.
